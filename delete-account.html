Delete Your Account

You can delete your Tunecrowd account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app or by contacting us.

How to delete from the app

1
Open Tunecrowd and sign in to your account
2
Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen
3
Scroll down and tap Delete Account
4
Confirm when prompted — your account and data will be permanently deleted immediately

What gets deleted

Email address & login credentials Deleted immediately
Profile (name, stats) Deleted immediately
Favourite stations Deleted immediately
Following & followers list Deleted immediately
Push notification token Deleted immediately
Chat messages in rooms Anonymised, not deleted
